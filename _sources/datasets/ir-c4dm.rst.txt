Description
^^^^^^^^^^^

This collection of room impulse responses was measured in the Great Hall, the Octagon, and a classroom at the Mile End campus of Queen Mary, University of London in 2008. The measurements were created using the sine sweep technique with a Genelec 8250A loudspeaker and two microphones, an omnidirectional DPA 4006 and a B-format Soundfield SPS422B. Only the omnidirectional recordings are included in ths=is release. When using any portion of this data set, cite: Stewart, Rebecca and Sandler, Mark. "Database of Omnidirectional and B-Format Impulse Responses", in Proc. of IEEE Int. Conf. on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing (ICASSP 2010), Dallas, Texas, March 2010.
